Activities

Research:

I am working as Ph.D fellow in Numerical Analysis group in Delft Institute of Applied Mathematics (DIAM) from March 2009. My research intrest is in numerical linear algebra in general, iterative solvers for PDEs in particular. Recently I have worked on developing robust and fast solvers for the Helmholtz equation, which involves the CSLP preconditioners and a variety of "deflation preconditioners". My daily supervisors are associate professor dr.ir. Domenico Lahaye , my promotors is Prof. dr. ir C. Vuik.
