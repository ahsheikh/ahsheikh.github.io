Helmholtz ADEF1 solver software

Here the software implementing our multilevel ADEF1 solver is given (together with a manual) to solve the two-dimensional Marmousi Problem. The solver part of software is implemented in PETSc whereas matrices are built in Matlab. For further instruction, see [Manual]
